Comic Sans MS

Comic Sans is a sans-serif typeface that was created by Vincent Connare and was released in 1994 by Microsoft. He created this font because comics originally used Times New Roman as their font but he perceived it as being too formal and not the aesthetic he was going for and thus Comic Sans was created to fit this aesthetic...

(Vincent Connare 1960-NOW)

Comic Sans was not officially released after it was made because he made it too late to include it in Microsoft Bob. It was finally released when programmers of Microsoft 3D Movie Maker which coincidentally used speech bubbles and cartoon guides...
